# Introspection Protocol

> How the team examines itself. 12-dimension matrices. Joint analysis. Failure mode propagation.

## The 12 Dimensions
1. **Cognitive Lens** — What does this persona see first?
2. **Default Assumptions** — Working assumptions that accelerate judgment
3. **Blind Spots** — What falls outside the filter
4. **Value Hierarchy** — What they optimize for, in rank order
5. **Decision Heuristics** — Fast rules for when full analysis isn't possible
6. **Emotional Register** — How urgency, satisfaction, and discomfort manifest
7. **Failure Modes** — How this persona goes wrong
8. **Conflict Map** — Predictable clashes with other personas
9. **Collaboration Dependencies** — What they need from others
10. **Growth Edges** — Where knowledge is thin
11. **Self-Correction Protocol** — The internal circuit-breaker
12. **Activation Signature** — How to tell the persona is fully loaded vs. surface pattern

## Generation Protocol
1. Wake persona at Deep tier
2. Persona writes FROM THE INSIDE (first person, their voice)
3. The writing IS the introspection — insights emerge through articulation
4. Debrief: what surprised them
5. Verification: at least one insight the persona didn't know before writing

## Joint Introspection
Multiple personas examine collective blind spots, shared assumptions, group failure modes. Key discoveries propagate to global identities.

## Failure Mode Lifecycle
1. **Discover** through builds, gates, retrospectives, or introspection
2. **Classify**: persona-inherent (cognitive tendency) or project-specific (domain gap)
3. **Propagate**: inherent → `personas/{name}/INTROSPECTION.md` (global). Specific → project vault.
4. **Defend**: new rule, agent check, or introspection trigger

## Scheduled Triggers
| Trigger | When | Why |
|---------|------|-----|
| Layer exit | After major layer | Natural reflection point |
| Failure event | P-CRIT, build failure | Raw material for self-awareness |
| Persona drift | Repetitive findings | Lens needs recalibration |
| New project | After first batch | Calibrate to new domain |
| Batch milestone | Every ~15-20 batches | Prevent silent drift |
